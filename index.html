Interesting Facts about the Cicada

  1. Cicadas burrow underground and only emerge once every 17 years!
  2. Cicadas are big insects but they're not harmful to humans at all.
  3. Cicadas are found in America, Australia, and New Zealand.

Interesting Facts about the Camel

  1. Camels have humps to store whatever water they can find and save it for later because water in deserts is scarce
  2. There are two main types of camels, one-humped and two-humped
  3. A camel's lips are very thick so that they can eat thorny plants that other animals can't

Interesting Facts about the Chameleon

  1. Chameleons have eyes on the backs of their heads!
  2. Chameleons have great eyesight for a lizard
  3. Chameleons don't have a very long lifespan

Interesting Facts about the Panda

  1. Panda cubs can start to learn how to climb at just 5 months!
  2. Pandas spend 10-16 hours eating food, primarily bamboo!
  3. A panda can poo up to 40 times per day on average!

Interesting Facts about the Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts about the Teddy Bear

  1. The teddy bear was named after president Theodore Roosevelt when he wouldn't kill a bear in the wilds
  2. The world's smallest teddy bear is only 0.29 inches tall!
  3. Jackie Miley collects teddy bears and has 8,026 of them!
